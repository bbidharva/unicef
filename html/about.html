UNICEF ensures every child has the chance to thrive, everywhere

UNICEF has been at the forefront of transforming children's lives for over 75 years, reaching the most vulnerable in over 190 countries. From providing lifesaving vaccines and clean water to implementing children's education and protection, we work tirelessly in the world's toughest places to give assistance to over 50 million children in crisis and reduce nature child mortality rates through innovative healthcare solutions. Together, with your support, we continue to create a world where every child can grow, thrive, and fulfill their potential.

1946
UNICEF is established to provide emergency relief to children affected by World War II in Europe and Asia.
1953
UNICEF becomes a permanent part of the United Nations system, focusing on long-term child welfare.
2000
UNICEF leads efforts toward the Millennium Development Goals, prioritizing child survival, education, and poverty reduction.
2010
UNICEF launches innovative mobile technology and education platforms and mobile health solutions to reach the most vulnerable children.
Present Day
UNICEF continues to address global challenges through conflict, and inequality to ensure that every child can survive, thrive, and reach their full potential.
